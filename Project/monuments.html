Places To Visit In Hyderabad Tour

Qutub Shahi Tombs

Ancient and historical landmarks with a mixture of Persian, Pashto, and Indian architectural styles.

Sindh Museum

A treasure trove of artifacts showcasing the history and culture of Sindh, including ancient coins, pottery, traditional clothing, and jewelry.

Shrine of Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai

A revered Sufi poet, mystic, and saint's shrine, known for his iconic collection of poetry.

Ghulam Nabi Kalhoro

Tomb Of Mian Ghulam Nabi Kalhoro is another iconic landmark that attracts those who want to experience the faith's evolution and perfection in this district.

Mohenjo Daro

The Archaeological Ruins at Moenjodaro are the best preserved urban settlement in South Asia dating back to the beginning of the 3rd millennium BC.

Tombs of Mirs

The tombs are also known as Cubbas (the Sindhi word for Domes). These tombs are located in Hirabad, Hyderabad in the Sindh province of Pakistan.
