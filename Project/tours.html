Tower Market is one the oldest areas of the city. Fruit and vegetable vendors and shopkeepers earn their bread and butter from dawn to dusk.

Kotri Barrage

Kotri Barrage, also known as the Ghulam Muhammad Barrage, is a barrage on the Indus River between Jamshoro and Hyderabad in the Sindh province of Pakistan.

Tombs of Mirs

The tombs are also known as Cubbas (the Sindhi word for Domes). These tombs are located in Hirabad, Hyderabad in the Sindh province of Pakistan.
